Two visions, one dream
A concept that was born over a passionate conversation about great style and design led to the origin of The Black Egg.

The founders combined their love for the culinary arts and tasteful creations to create the ideal spot nestled away in the heart of Ta' Xbiex. Introducing a refined brunch concept to the island had been a lifelong dream of theirs, and what was once no more than a spontaneous dish suggestion became The Black Egg's signature dish - a poached black egg itself - that led to the brand's inception.

Brunch and Dinner

Brunch is served all day in an upbeat environment and chic interiors, making it the go-to spot for catching up over a light meal and Prosecco. Larger bites and scrumptious lunches are also on offer, however, satisfying any palate.

Dinners, which are accompanied by a selection of prestigious wines, are available on select days.
